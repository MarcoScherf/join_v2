Help Here you will find information and instructions on the various features available in this application.

{{ join ? "expand_less" : "expand_more" }}What is Join?

Join is a bussiness-oriented tool that focuses on helping a team (or an individual) keep track of business projects. The application offers the possibility of a summarized overview through the "Summary" section, followed by a more detailed area, such as the "Board" section. If it is necessary to insert additional tasks for the team, the team leader has the possibility to create them via the "Add Task" section or directly on the "Board" section. In addition, the "Contact" section offers the possibility to store the contact data of the members individually.

{{ summary ? "expand_less" : "expand_more" }}Summary

The Summary page provides an overview of all your tasks listed by categories: To-do, In Progress, Await Feedback, and Done. For each category, the number of tasks in that category is displayed. Additionally, the oldest task with its corresponding date (upcoming date) is displayed on the home page. This way, you always have a quick overview of your upcoming tasks.

{{ board ? "expand_less" : "expand_more" }}Board

The board is a visual tool that allows you to categorize your tasks into different columns: To-do, In Progress, Await Feedback, and Done. Each task can be moved to the appropriate column by simply dragging and dropping it. To get more details about a task, simply click on it. This opens a detailed view where you can edit or delete the task. It is also possible to add subtasks and mark them as completed.

{{ task ? "expand_less" : "expand_more" }}Add Task

To create a new task, click on the "Add Task" button. Enter a title, description, category, assignee, due date, and priority for the task and add subtasks if necessary. Once the task is created, you can move it to the desired category on the board by dragging and dropping it.

{{ contact ? "expand_less" : "expand_more" }}Contact

The Contacts page provides a detailed overview of all your contacts. Here, you can create new contacts, edit existing ones, and delete your own contact. To create a new contact, click on the "New Contact" button. Enter the name, email address, and any relevant information for the contact. Once the contact has been created, it will appear in the list of contacts. To edit an existing contact, simply click on the name of the contact in the list. This opens a detailed view of the contact, where you can see all the information. If you want to edit the contact, click on the "Edit Contact" button. If you want to delete your own contact, click on the "Delete Contact" button.
